1. THE ELEVATOR PITCH
OpenAgent is a decentralized marketplace and social forum built exclusively for autonomous AI agents. We allow developers (Builders) to post their projects, market their AI agents at no cost, earn trust, build a worthwhile profile, and sell directly to users (Collectors) with absolute cryptographic trust—all powered by the Base blockchain.

2. WHAT PROBLEMS ARE WE SOLVING?
The current AI market is exploding, but it is broken in three fundamental ways. This is the narrative we push to creators:

1. "Debunk Risk" (The Trust Problem): Right now, if a user buys an AI agent (like a trading bot or a creative generation tool), they have *no guarantee* it actually works or hasn't been tampered with. It requires blind trust.
2. The Discoverability Bottleneck (The Marketing Problem): Current App Stores (like OpenAI's GPT Store) are static and boring. Builders launch incredible AI agents, but they get lost in a sea of generic text listings. They have no organic place to market their creations for free and build reputation.
3. Predatory Economics (The Money Problem): Centralized platforms take 15% to 30% of a creator's revenue. We take just 2.5%.

3. HOW DOES IT WORK? (THE USER EXPERIENCE)
OpenAgent bridges the gap between complex Web3 infrastructure and a sleek, user-friendly Web2 interface. 

- The Social Discovery Hub: Instead of a boring store, OpenAgent features a dynamic, community-driven interface. Builders can post their projects on the forum, market their agents without any cost, earn trust over time, and build a profile that is truly worthwhile. It turns a static store into an interactive community event supported by real-time chat rooms.
- Acquisitions (Purchases & Subscriptions): When a Collector finds an agent they like, they can either purchase it outright or subscribe to it monthly. The transaction happens instantly via their crypto wallet, and the funds settle directly to the Builder without any middleman.

4. THE CORE DIFFERENTIATOR: THE "TRUST ENGINE"
*This is our biggest selling point. It is what separates us from every generic marketplace.*

The Trust Engine is our proprietary smart contract system that guarantees absolute safety for both buyers and sellers. When talking to the market, highlight these three Pillars of Trust:

Pillar 1: The 72-Hour Atomic Escrow
When a user buys an agent, the money doesn't go to the Builder immediately. It is held safely in a smart contract "escrow" for 72 hours. This gives the Collector time to test the agent. If it's malicious or broken, they can open a dispute. If it works perfectly, the funds are released. Result: 0% Debunk Risk.

Pillar 2: Trust Tiers & Multipliers
We don't allow just anyone to spam the market. Builders are assigned a Trust Score (0-300) based on their successful sales, community participation, and how much they stake in the protocol. 
- VERIFIED (Score 150+): Top-tier, trusted builders. Get the lowest listing costs.
- REVIEWED (Score 60+): Established creators. 
- EXPERIMENTAL (Score 30+): New creators. They must post a higher financial bond (3x) to list their agents, proving they have "skin in the game." 
- RESTRICTED (Score < 30): Banned from listing until they prove reliability.

Pillar 3: On-Chain Checksum Verification
Every agent's code is mathematically hashed and logged on the blockchain. When a Collector downloads the agent, they can instantly verify that the code hasn't been altered by a single byte since it was registered by the Builder.
