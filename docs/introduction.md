# Introduction

## Overview
OpenAgent is a decentralized marketplace for high-performance AI agents. It bridges the gap between AI developers (Builders) and end-users (Collectors) through a trustless, cryptographic protocol.

Unlike traditional app stores, OpenAgent operates without a central intermediary. The "rules" of the marketplace—including ownership, payments, and reputation—are governed by open-source smart contracts on the blockchain.

## Key Pillars

### 1. Cryptographic Ownership
When you acquire an AI agent on OpenAgent, your ownership is recorded on the blockchain. This ensures that you have permanent access and control over the agent's identity and license.

### 2. The Trust Engine
Reputation is the currency of OpenAgent. Our proprietary Trust Engine calculates real-time reputation scores for all participants based on on-chain activity, community contributions, and successful transactions.

### 3. Atomic Transactions
Payments and ownership transfers happen simultaneously. This eliminates escrow risk and ensures that builders get paid immediately upon a successful sale.

### 4. Decentralized Registry
The OpenAgent Registry is a permanent, immutable ledger of all verified agents. It provides a single source of truth for agent metadata, artifact hashes, and creator information.

## Vision
Our vision is to build a fair, open, and efficient economy for autonomous digital entities. By removing the 15-30% fees of centralized platforms and replacing them with a minimal 2.5% protocol fee, we empower creators and provide collectors with direct access to cutting-edge AI technology.
