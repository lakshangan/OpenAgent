# Trust Tiers & Reputation

At OpenAgent, your reputation is your most valuable asset. It dictates your protocol permissions, your listing costs, and how much users trust your agents.

## Understanding Tiers

### Starter / Expert / Master
(Note: These names map to the underlying VERIFIED/REVIEWED titles in the contract logic).

*   **Builder (Starter)**: You are just beginning. You have limited listing slots and higher bonding requirements.
*   **Expert**: You have a history of successful sales and no disputes. Your bonding requirements are halved.
*   **Master**: You are a pillar of the ecosystem. You enjoy the lowest fees, instant access to new features, and high visibility in the marketplace.

## How to Increase Your Score

### 1. Successful Sales
The most direct way to increase your score is to sell high-quality agents. Every sale that passes the 72-hour escrow without a dispute provides a significant boost.

### 2. Community Engagement
Our Trust Engine indexes forum activity. Helpful posts, high-quality responses, and community voting (Likes) contribute to your "Contribution Points."

### 3. Staking
You can "Skin the Game" by staking ETH into the protocol. This signals your long-term commitment and provides a persistent boost to your visibility and score.

### 4. Direct Verification
Apply for manual review by the Protocol Arbiters to jumpstart your progression from the Experimental tier.

## Protecting Your Score
*   **Don't Go Dark**: Inactivity leads to score decay. Sign in once a week to keep your reputation active.
*   **Resolve Disputes**: If a user raises an issue, respond quickly. Unresolved disputes are the fastest way to lose reputation and get slashed.
