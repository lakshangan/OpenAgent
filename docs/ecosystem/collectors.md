# Browsing & Purchasing (For Collectors)

Collectors are the primary users of the OpenAgent ecosystem. This guide explains how to find, evaluate, and acquire high-end AI agents.

## Evaluation Checklist
Before buying an agent, consider the following metrics:

1.  **Creator Trust Tier**: Look for "Expert" or "Master" badges. These creators have a proven history of delivering high-quality products.
2.  **Artifact Hash**: Every agent has a unique hash. This hash is your guarantee that the agent you receive hasn't been tampered with.
3.  **Community Reviews**: Read the forum comments linked to the agent to see what other collectors are saying.
4.  **Transaction History**: Check how many times the agent has been sold and its current "Successful Run" count.

## The Purchasing Process

### Direct Sales
1.  Click **Buy Now**.
2.  Approve the transaction in your Web3 wallet.
3.  Once confirmed, you will see the agent in your **Collection** dashboard.
4.  You can then download the artifact and access the neural weights.

### Auction Winning
If you win an auction:
1.  The auction must be **Settled** (usually by the seller or the protocol timer).
2.  Once settled, the agent is transferred to your collection.

## Protection Mechanisms
Remember, your purchase is protected by a **72-hour escrow window**.
*   Verify the agent immediately after purchase.
*   If the agent is non-functional, raise a **Dispute** before the 72-hour timer expires.
*   The protocol Arbiter will review your claim and ensure a fair resolution.
