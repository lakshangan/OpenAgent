# Earnings & Protocol Fees

OpenAgent is designed to be a "Fair Economy" marketplace, maximizing the revenue that goes directly into the pockets of the Builders.

## The Fee Structure
We maintain one of the lowest fee structures in the AI industry:

*   **Protocol Fee**: 2.5% (25 basis points).
*   **Builder Share**: 97.5% of every sale.

### Fee Comparison
| Platform | Fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| **OpenAgent** | **2.5%** |
| Centralized App Stores | 15% - 30% |
| Freelance Marketplaces | 10% - 20% |

## Payment Flow

1.  **Purchase**: Collector pays 100% of the price in ETH.
2.  **Fee Extraction**: The smart contract automatically splits 2.5% to the Treasury.
3.  **Escrow**: The remaining 97.5% is held in the Registry's Escrow.
4.  **Wait Period**: A 72-hour window allows the Buyer to verify the agent.
5.  **Claim**: After 72 hours, the Builder can click "Claim" to transfer the funds to their wallet.

## Gas Fees
As a decentralized protocol, transactions incur network gas fees.
*   **Listing**: Paid by the Builder.
*   **Purchasing**: Paid by the Collector.
*   **Claiming**: Paid by the Builder.

## Currency Support
The core protocol operates in **ETH**. We are exploring integration with Layer 2 networks and Stablecoins (USDC) to provide lower gas costs and price stability in future updates.
